Welcome to El Camino.

At El Camino General Engineering Inc (El Camino), you can expect to deal with friendly people that will offer you great prices. We are a family owned business that believes in maintaining the roads and surfaces that take you to your destination; and if we can’t build you a road, we can surely maintain it. We believe in transparency and efficiency to create cost effective solutions to help with your current needs.

The founder of El Camino has over 30 years of local experience in the paving industry. He began his career as a Construction Laborer for various Paving Companies in the Bay Area during the late 80’s and early 90’s. He has now partnered with his Son who has an educational background in Civil Engineering, and years of experience working for Special Inspection Agencies, General Building Contractors, and Private Land Developers to develop solutions that work… Together, they form a team that develop innovative solutions!

Services

People require businesses to provide their respective services with minimal impact to routine business operations and the environment.
